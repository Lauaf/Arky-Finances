How to fill this section

Use the labels the planner needs, not the labels a bank statement gives you.

Fixed costs

Use this for rent, insurance, subscriptions, and debt payments.

If the amount is difficult to avoid month to month, it belongs in fixed expenses.

Variable costs

Use this for groceries, fuel, takeout, leisure, and shopping.

This is the part of the plan you can usually adjust when the month gets tight.

One-off hits

Use one-time expenses for exceptions.

Medical bills, repairs, or one-off purchases should not repeat through every future month.
