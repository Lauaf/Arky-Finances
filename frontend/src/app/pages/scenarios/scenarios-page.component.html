How to fill this section

Use scenarios to challenge the plan, not to predict the future perfectly.

Conservative

Make this one uncomfortable.

Higher inflation and bigger surprise expenses reveal how quickly your monthly margin disappears.

Base

This should be your planning default.

Use the assumptions you genuinely expect most of the time, not the most convenient ones.

Optimistic

Use upside carefully.

Better returns and lower inflation are useful for comparison, but they should not drive daily decisions.
