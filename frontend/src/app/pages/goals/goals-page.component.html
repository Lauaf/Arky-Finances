How to fill this section

Keep goals concrete enough that the planner can defend the recommendation.

One goal, one outcome

Do not bundle everything into one big savings bucket.

Separate your emergency fund, travel plan, and major purchases so priorities stay visible.

Priority

Lower numbers should matter more.

Use priority 1 for goals that deserve money first when the month becomes tight.

Ideal contribution

Think of this as the pace you want, not a promise.

The recommender uses it as guidance, then checks if the month can actually support it.
